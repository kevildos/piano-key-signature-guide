Piano Key Signature Guide

A web tool created to help familiarize people with key signatures. To learn more about key signatures, click here.

Try selecting a key in the column on the right to see it displayed on the piano. The highlighted keys in green are the TONIC, or the root notes. The red are the DOMINANT notes and the yellow denote the rest of the notes in the scale.

If you want to find out what key signature a particular signature of notes are in, click "Record", play the notes, and click stop to see what key signatures the melody could be in.

Try playing the highlighted keys focusing on the dominant and tonic notes to produce more of a melodic sound!
